Features

Well thought out to give you exactly what you need. No bloat.

Text Only for Employees

No apps to install, works on every mobile platform with SMS. Perfect for areas with bad data signals. Doesnt require employees to have an expensive smart phone.

Comfirmation emails & texts

Employers get confirmation emails and texts when a shift is filled. No need to sit around the office waiting for responses.

Instant Reach

Because OpenShift is powered by SMS it'll work anywhere in the world that has cell service. Employees are added by phone number. No need for them to install an app, sign up, and configure their account. Just text and go.

Track Shifts

Comprehensive analytics give you detailed stats about how your open shifts are being filled.

Fast

Sign up and manage your open shifts through any smartphone. An employee response is as fast as a single reply.

Very Affordable

Easy to manage, far reaching, and brilliantly quick for employees to use, all at a low monthly rate.
